Proof of Concept — Subdomain Takeover

This page demonstrates a harmless proof-of-concept for a takeover of SUBDOMAIN.EXAMPLE.COM. It does not access, modify, or exfiltrate any data. It simply shows that control of the domain can be gained.

Observed: DNS CNAME points to an unclaimed external service (e.g., some-service.provider.net), allowing provisioning of content.
